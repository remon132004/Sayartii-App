Abstract This project develops a Predictive Vehicle Maintenance System using On-Board Diagnostics (OBD) and machine learning to enhance vehicle reliability, safety, and efficiency. The system aims to predict vehicle issues before failures occur, shifting from reactive to proactive maintenance. It analyzes vehicle data to identify potential problems and notifies users in real-time, reducing unexpected breakdowns and promoting safer driving.Our approach includes data collection from OBD-II devices, machine learning algorithms for prediction, and a mobile app for real-time alerts. The system effectively predicts maintenance needs, reducing breakdowns and enhancing preventive maintenance, which improves vehicle longevity and reduces emissions. The project has the potential to transform traditional vehicle maintenance, lower operational costs, and improve road safety. Designed for compatibility across various car models, it facilitates early detection and maintenance alerts, setting the stage for advancements in predictive vehicle maintenance.
